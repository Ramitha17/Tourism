About Chennai

Chennai is a city of history and modernity. It is home to Marina Beach, the world’s second-longest urban beach, as well as ancient temples and vibrant culture.

Culture

Chennai is the heart of Tamil culture, known for Bharatanatyam dance, Carnatic music, and intricate temple architecture.

Language

The official language of Chennai is Tamil, one of the world's oldest languages with a rich literary history.

Festivals

Pongal is Chennai’s biggest festival, celebrated over four days with traditional rituals, feasts, and joy.

Experience Chennai

From its ancient temples to modern cityscapes, Chennai offers something for every traveler. Start exploring today!
